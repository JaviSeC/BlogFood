RECIPES:

Arepa

Recipe

Ingredients:

      300 ml. of warm water..
      220 g. of P.A.N. or similar precooked corn flour
      1/2 dessert spoonful of salt (4 grams)

PISCA ANDINA

Recipe

Ingredients:

      4 cups of liquid milk or 1L.
      4 tablespoons salted butter (use butter if possible, not margarine).
      3 scallion stalks 150 g.
      4 medium potatoes 400 g.
      1 coriander bouquet.
      250 g. of white cheese, the original recipe is made with smoked white cheese.
      4 eggs.
      Salt to taste.

PANQUECAS

Recipe

Ingredients:

      1 cup wheat flour.
      2 eggs.
      2 tazas de leche líquida.
      ½ cup liquid butter.
      2 spoonfuls of sugar.
      ½ cup oil..
      300 g of grated cheese.

TEQUEÑOS

Recipe

Ingredients:

      175 g of wheat flour.
      30 g cold butter.
      1 egg.
      35 ml of water.
      1 pinch of salt.
      200 g of white cheese type llanero (if not gouda) cut in sticks of 2 cm wide and 8 cm long
      500 g frying oilr

EMPANADAS

Recipe

Ingredients:

      1 1/2 cup (215 grams) precooked corn flour.
      2 tablespoons all-purpose flour or cornstarch.
      1/4 teaspoon salt.
      1 1/2 cups of cold water.

PASTELES

Recipe

Ingredients:

      500 g sifted wheat flour.
      250 ml of water (or more).
      1 teaspoon salt.
      1 sachet of baking powder.
      1 tablespoon neutral vegetable oil (or melted margarine).

GOLFEADOS

Recipe

Ingredients:

      1 teaspoon of yeast.
      4 tablespoons sugar.
      1 tablespoon butter.
      2 tablespoons of salt
      ½ kilogram of flour.
      2 cups of water.
      1 Papelon.
      Melted butter.
      Grated cheese.
